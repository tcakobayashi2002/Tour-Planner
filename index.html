    Cultural Insights 🏮

    Local Etiquette

    When visiting temples or pagodas, ensure your shoulders and knees are covered. Remove your shoes before entering someone's home.

    Street Food Guide

    Follow the locals! Eat where it's crowded. Try Pho for breakfast, Banh Mi for lunch, and finish with a refreshing iced coffee with condensed milk (Ca phe sua da).

    Crossing the Street

    Walk slowly and steadily. Do not stop abruptly or step back; motorbikes will naturally flow around you.
